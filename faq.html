Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

If your question isn't here, the strategy call is the fastest way to get a real answer.

Who do you coach?

I work with CEOs, founders, and senior executives who want sustainable peak performance — the kind that compounds for decades, not just quarters. The common thread is ambition and a willingness to do real work in private.

How long is a typical engagement?

Most clients work with me in 6- to 12-month containers with weekly 1:1 sessions, plus on-demand strategic support between sessions. Shorter sprints are available for specific projects on request.

Do you coach in person or virtually?

I work with executives globally via secure video. In-person intensives are available for retainer clients on request — we travel to you or meet in a curated location.

What makes your approach different?

Most coaching focuses on one lever — mindset, strategy, or wellness. I integrate all three because that's how compounding happens. You don't get a coach who specializes in one thing; you get an operating system.

What's the investment?

Programs start at premium-tier pricing reflective of the access, customization, and outcomes involved. Specific numbers are shared on the strategy call once we've confirmed mutual fit.

Is everything confidential?

Yes. Every engagement is governed by mutual NDA. Client identities, conversations, and materials are never shared. This is non-negotiable and central to how the work operates.

Can you provide references?

Yes. Verified references are available to qualified inquiries after a fit call. Out of respect for confidentiality, we don't publish names or share references publicly.

How quickly can we start?

Most engagements begin within 2–4 weeks of the strategy call, depending on calendar availability. Cohort openings are limited and typically announced via the announcement bar on this site.

What if I'm not the right fit?

The strategy call exists to determine that — for both of us. If we're not a fit, you'll leave with clear next-step recommendations regardless. There's zero pressure on either side.

How do I get started?

Book a confidential 30-minute strategy call. We'll diagnose your highest-leverage shift and decide together if a working relationship is the right fit.
