My Own Practice

What I actually do.
Daily. Without exception.

I do not coach what I do not practice. The rhythms below are mine, not theory. They are also the foundation of every wellness engagement.

Morning

Before the inbox

Outdoor walk, twenty minutes, no phone. Stillness, ten minutes. Real breakfast with protein. Day intentions written by hand. Total: 50 to 60 minutes.

Midday

Recovery as default

Meals away from the desk. Buffer between meetings. One outdoor break in the middle of the day. Three sessions of strength work per week.

Evening

Closing the day

One screen-free hour before sleep. Fixed bedtime within a 60-minute window. Sundays fully work-free. The recovery that allows the rest.
